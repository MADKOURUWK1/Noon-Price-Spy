Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2025

This privacy policy explains how Noon Price Spy V2 ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Use

To provide email alerts, we use Google's authentication services. If you choose to connect your Gmail account, we require access to the following permissions:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email: We use this permission to verify your identity and display your email address within the extension's settings. This confirms that you are connected.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send: This permission is used only to send price alert emails to you, from your own email account. We do not read, delete, or manage your email in any other way. The extension automatically sends an email on your behalf only when a product you are tracking has a price change that meets your alert criteria.

How We Use Your Information

Your Google user information is used exclusively to provide the optional email alert functionality of the extension. We do not store your personal data on our servers, and we do not sell or share your information with any third parties. All product and tracking information is stored locally on your computer through Chrome's Storage API.
